Challenge Solved
The blockchain first addresses the cost of trust, which can significantly reduce transaction costs. In the voting field, the one-person-one-vote mechanism and the authority restrictions can be very effective in improving the trustworthiness of voting results.
At present, the blockchain voting is still in the development stage, and it is not mature in the true sense and needs constant testing. Japan, Russia and Switzerland have conducted large-scale tests using the blockchain voting system. Future technological breakthroughs may bring the voting system to the national level.
